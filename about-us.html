Barlows About Us 关于我们

Barlows is proud to be a New Zealand owned and operated company with over 10000 square metres of warehouse capability, dovetailing into a comprehensive distribution network managed by our teams in Auckland and Wellington. Over the past 20 years, we have secured an impressive portfolio of clients by responding to their changing needs and growing with them into the valued freight and logistics partner that we are today. Our focus on customer service is locked into every aspect of our service, as is achieving ongoing customer satisfaction in what we do and the manner in which we do it. Open channels of communication and a hands-on style of management ensure a friendly and relaxed way of doing business with our clients. We have enormous faith in our people, and in particular in their capabilities in specialist areas such as warehousing, distribution and transport solutions. We deliver - so you can deliver.
